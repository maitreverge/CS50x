Dropshipping, Ins & Out's

Dropshipping is a business model in which a retailer (the dropshipper) sells products to customers without having to keep those products in stock. Instead, when a dropshipper receives an order from a customer, they simply purchase the product from a third-party supplier or wholesaler who then directly ships the product to the customer.

In other words, this kind of businesses often plays on the custommer ignorance of websites such as :
